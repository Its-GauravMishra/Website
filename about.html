About RR Industries

India's No.1 Unique Earthing Company

Our Story

RR Industries is a leading manufacturer of high-quality earthing solutions in India. We specialize in producing GI Earthing Electrodes, Pure Copper Earthing Electrodes, Copper Bonded Earthing Rods, and Earthing Compounds that set the industry standard for safety and reliability.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every product we manufacture. With ISO 9001:2015 certification and NABL-tested solutions, we ensure that each earthing system we deliver provides optimal protection against electrical faults and lightning strikes.
